# Sample knowledge entry

This is a sample file showing how the AI knowledge folder works. Replace or delete it with your
own content.

Anything written here becomes searchable context for the assistant after you reindex. For
example, you could add company-specific facts the help section doesn't cover, such as:

- Office hours are 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
- The HR helpdesk can be reached at hr@yourcompany.example.
- New employees should complete onboarding within their first week.
